About

A vampire story where all the main characters are queer!

Summary

A wandering, recently turned, vampire struggles to survive in San Francisco. Despite trying her best to minding her own damn business, a renegade hunter, a young savant witch and a master vampire are all keen in using her for her ‘unique’ ability. Each have their own reasons to use her. However, they all get embroiled in a battle for an ancient powerful relic that could mean the end of all vampires or the end of all humanity.
